Quanta Coffee

At Quanta, we truly understand the coffee process from the seed to your cup.

We are a unique coffee company that offers distinguished Specialty Coffees, right from the Colombian Coffee Triangle. We are not middlemen; we are a team of coffee farmers, roasters, and distributors, who together have made this brand to highlight Specialty Coffees from this exclusive region of Colombia. We focus on having small batches of extremely differentiated coffees. We offer coffees from our own farms as well as coffees from other local farms, who we work hand in hand with. These notable coffees have yet to open a sustainable market so we encourage and accompany them in the use of the processes known to create Specialty Coffees.

We are constantly focusing on how we can bring a better cup of coffee to our customers. Working year-round in Colombia assures our quality is nothing less than perfect.

We invite you to personally follow us on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, Youtube and Snapchat, where you can see the full transparency in all of our processes, farms, and social responsibility.

Taste the diference!

Mateo Hoyos

Mr. Mateo Hoyos Bedoya has been the Founder and Executive Director of Quanta Coffee since August 1st, 2015 when the company first started. After graduating with a law degree from the Von Humbolt University of Quindio in Colombia, Mateo immersed himself in the field of commodities, particularly coffee. He has 2 years of experience in both the public and the private sectors, including his tenure as both the Director of the Coffee Department in the Quindio Colombia, Livestock and Food Supply and the Commodities Secretary in the Colombia Ministry of Development.

Juan Pablo Giraldo

Juan Pablo Giraldo has worked in the technology industry for over 7 years focusing primarily on innovation and development of Information Technology. Juan has designed the web page for Quanta Coffee and also helps manage the structure of company on a day to day basis. His experience and value to the company is essential.

Max Shapiro

Max Shapiro is a Co-Founder of Quanta Coffee and is currently the Chief Financial Officer. Max grew up in South Florida and now lives on a small island, also known as, Manhattan. Max received his education in Business at Binghamton University. He has worked for large finance and private equity firms in the New York City area. He helps Quanta Coffee in many different areas but focuses primarily on finances, sales, and human resources. He has been drinking coffee for quite some time now and truly appreciates the quality that Quanta Coffee brings to the table.

HARRIS MARKOWITZ

Harris Markowitz an avid coffee drinker is a full-time Snapchat filmmaker and owner of Snapchat production company, A Cereal Production. Being an old friend of Mateo, and Max he decided to work with us to promote our initial Kickstarter campaign, as well as consulting on the management of our Snapchat. An expert on the social space, Markowitz sees Snapchat as everyone’s own personal TV station, giving them the freedom to choose who and what they watch on their own time.

Harris Wellner

Harris Wellner is the Chief Operating Officer at Quanta. Hailing from Long Island, New York, Harris comes from a long line of food entrepreneurs. Prior to joining Quanta, Harris received his Masters in Accounting from Binghamton University, and worked at one of the largest public accounting firms in New York City. Now, Harris combines his passion for international business with his family history in food importing to bring coffee from our Colombian farms to you as fast as possible by heading logistics and online sales efforts. Harris honestly admits that before Quanta he really didn't enjoy the taste of coffee. However, Quanta has given him a new view on the way coffee is supposed to taste and he cannot get enough. He has turned over to the dark side, enjoying his coffee black.

David Patrone

David Patrone is co-founder and CMO of Quanta Coffee. After being born and raised in New Jersey, he made his way to SUNY Binghamton where he met fellow founders Max and Harris. After Binghamton David made a home for himself in Brooklyn where he quickly started to see the different appeals of local coffee shops in the area. David is responsible for building the Quanta Coffee brand and showing coffee lovers around the world why they should Taste The Difference.
